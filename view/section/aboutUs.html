About Us

  • Why Choose Core Path for Your Child's Journey?

At Core Path, we believe that every child deserves a meaningful education tailored to their needs, values, and aspirations. Here’s why Core Path is the perfect place for your child to grow and thrive:

  1. Holistic Education Rooted in Core Value

    2. Core Path is more than just a learning center—it’s a nurturing environment where we combine spiritual growth, academic excellence, and life skills. We help children and teens develop not only their minds but also their character and moral compass.


  2. Customized Learning for Every Age Group

    3. Our carefully designed programs cater to specific age groups:

    • Tiny Tilmiz (4-6 years): Laying the foundation of love for learning.

    • Young Explorers (7-8 years): Encouraging curiosity and critical thinking.

    • Future Builders (9-10 years): Building strong knowledge and skills.

    • Pathway Pioneers (11-13 years): Developing independence and leadership.

    • Aspiring Leaders (14-17 years): Preparing for real-world success.


  3. Expert Teachers and Personalized Attention

    4. Our experienced and compassionate teachers are dedicated to each student’s growth. With small class sizes and a personalized approach, every child gets the attention and guidance they need to succeed.


  4. Flexible Learning Options

    5. We offer both online and offline classes to accommodate different learning styles and schedules, ensuring your child can learn in the way that suits them best.


  5. Focus on Quranic and English Mastery

    6. Core Path blends Quranic education with English proficiency, helping your child excel academically while deepening their connection to their faith.


  6. Affordable and Transparent Pricing

    7. We’re committed to providing high-quality education at a price that’s accessible for families in Bandung and beyond.


  7. A Community That Cares

    8. By joining Core Path, your child becomes part of a vibrant and supportive community. We work closely with parents to ensure our students’ success, fostering a sense of belonging and shared purpose.
